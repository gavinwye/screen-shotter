Financial help if you have children

If you have a child there are certain benefits you may be able to claim.

These benefits are:

  • Child Benefit
  • Child Tax Credits
  • Universal Credit

Sign in with Government Gateway to check if you're eligible

Sign in

If you don't have a Government Gateway account you can create one.

You need a way to prove your identity. This can be one of the following:

  • a UK passport
  • details from any of your 3 most recent payslips
  • the last 4 digits of the account that your tax credits or pension is paid into
  • details from your P60

You will also need your National Insurance Number
