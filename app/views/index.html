Claim Child Benefit

You can claim Child Benefit if:

  • you are responsible for a child
  • the child is under 16 or between 16 and 20 and in full-time, non-advanced education
  • you have the right to reside in the UK
  • you earn less than £50,000 per year

To claim Child benefit online you need to login with your Government Gateway ID
