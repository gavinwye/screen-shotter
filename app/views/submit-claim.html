Submit your claim

When you submit your claim the following things will happen:

  • we will validate your claim. which usually takes between 7 - 14 days
  • once your claim has been validated, if it's successful it will be paid
  • we will write to you and tell you if your claim has been successful. This letter normally arrives after the money arrives in your bank account.
