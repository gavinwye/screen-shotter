Before you submit your claim

There are cirtan circumstances that will effect your claim.

You'll need to tell the Child Benefit office if any of the following things happen.

  • The child you are claiming for stops living with you more than 50% of the time
  • Banks details (account number and sort code)
  • address the account is registered to
  • You change address, including moving away from the UK
  • You start to earn more than £50,099
  • The child you are claiming for stops living with you for more than 50% of the time.
  • your bank account, name or address changes
  • a relationship ends
  • you start a new relationship, eg you move in with a new partner or remarry
  • the child’s parent has died
  • you intend to go abroad for more than 8 weeks
  • you or your partner intend to move abroad permanently or for more than a year
  • you move to or from Northern Ireland
  • you have a baby, or a child comes to live with you
  • payments or contributions for a child who doesn’t live with you stop or change
  • you’re paid to look after a child by a local council or similar
  • your immigration status changes
  • you lose the right to reside in the UK
  • you get a prison sentence of more than 8 weeks
